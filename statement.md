# Expense Tracker - Project Statement

## Problem Statement
Managing personal finances is challenging for many people due to lack of proper tracking and budgeting tools. Without a structured way to record daily expenses, individuals often overspend, lose track of their spending patterns, and struggle to make informed financial decisions.  

## Scope of the Project
The Expense Tracker aims to provide a simple yet effective solution for recording, categorizing, and analyzing personal expenses. It allows users to track spending over time, generate summaries, and maintain a secure history of transactions. The project serves as a foundation for developing advanced personal finance tools, such as budgeting apps or analytics dashboards.  

## Target Users
- Students who want to manage pocket money or small budgets  
- Professionals seeking better control over monthly expenses  
- Anyone looking for a simple and efficient way to track and categorize daily spending  

## High-Level Features
- Add, edit, and delete expense transactions  
- Categorize expenses (e.g., food, transport, bills, entertainment)  
- Daily, weekly, and monthly expense summaries  
- Secure storage of transaction history  
- Import and export data using CSV files  
- Basic analytics for monitoring spending trends
